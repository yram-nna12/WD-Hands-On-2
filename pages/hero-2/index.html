BAYMAX

ABOUT

Baymax, the healthcare companion from "Big Hero 6," excels in both medical support and combat scenarios. Equipped with state-of-the-art medical scanners and an array of treatment options, Baymax can swiftly diagnose and address injuries, ensuring allies are always in peak condition. His inflatable vinyl body provides exceptional durability and cushioning against impacts. In combat, Baymax uses his rocket fists and flight capabilities to deliver powerful blows and swift maneuvers. His tactical AI allows him to analyze battle conditions and adapt strategies on the fly, making him an invaluable asset in both healing and combat support roles..
