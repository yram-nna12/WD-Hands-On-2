AANG

ABOUT

Aang is the last Airbender and the Avatar, destined to master all four elements to maintain world balance. Discovered as the Avatar at 12, he was frozen in an iceberg for 100 years before awakening to end the Hundred Year War.

SKILLS

Waterbending: Proficient in manipulating water, ice, and healing techniques.

Earthbending: Trained by Toph, he can manipulate rocks and sense vibrations.

Firebending: Can generate and control fire, learned to use its power responsibly.

Avatar State: Accesses immense power and knowledge of past Avatars.
